About Me

Hello, I'm Projukta Chatterjee - a student of Integrated MTech in Data Science at VIT Bhopal. I'm an engineer by passion with a keen interest in data analysis, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

My journey in data science began with a curiosity about how data can tell stories and reveal patterns that help solve real-world problems. I believe that engineering is not just about technical skills, but also about creativity and innovation.

When I'm not coding or analyzing data, you can find me pursuing my hobbies which include drawing, crafting, swimming, and playing basketball. These activities help me maintain a balance between logical thinking and creative expression.

I'm passionate about using data science to create meaningful solutions and am always eager to learn new technologies and methodologies in this rapidly evolving field.
